Locam

Camera Access Required

Locam needs access to your camera to scan QR codes. Your camera feed is processed locally on your device - no data is sent to any server.

Camera Access Denied

To use Locam, you need to allow camera access. Follow these steps:

1

Check Firefox Permissions

Click the lock icon in the address bar and ensure camera permissions are set to "Allow"

2

Check System Settings

Make sure Firefox has camera access in your system settings (Privacy & Security)

3

Restart Firefox

If issues persist, try restarting Firefox and granting permission again

4

Check Camera Availability

Ensure your camera is connected and not being used by another application

Camera Access Granted

You can now close this tab and use the Locam extension to scan QR codes.
